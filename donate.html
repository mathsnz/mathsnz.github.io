I am a Maths teacher at a large school in New Zealand. This site has been set up in my own time to support maths teachers in NZ. The aim of the site is to provide resources and guides for teachers and students. All of these resources are provided free of charge... but donations are gladly accepted.

You can also check us out on Facebook to keep up to date with any new features or resources.

MathsNZ is the teacher focused size of this group of websites. Here you will find lots of resources to help you in your teaching, including online tools, booklets that you can print off, and various other different resources and ideas. See here for a full list of the different resources split up by standard / curriculum level.
NZGrapher is a free graphing tool that can run on any device, without an install. NZGrapher was developed to run on anything with a browser, computers, iPads, ChromeBooks, Microsoft Surface, Android, even iPhones.
Now available for download for PC and for hosting at your school... more info here.
MathsNZ Students is a place for students to go to get online lessons and related questions... still under development, and more resources are being added regularly.
MathsNZ Question Generator is a free tool to generate an endless stream of maths questions... new question types are being added regularly, so if you have a good idea please let me know.
