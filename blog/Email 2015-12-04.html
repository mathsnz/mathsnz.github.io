NZGrapher Updates
A whole bunch of changes have been made in the last few months:
  • Lots of bug fixes
  • Points are now easier to see on most graphs, and the colours now go through the whole rainbow rather than just red to green.
  • You can bootstrap single variables (see picture to the right)
  • Time series now allows you to choose between multiplicative and additive models
  • Added ability to create a new calculated column (under "Sample and More").
  • Blank / non-numerical points in re-randomisation and bootstraps are no longer plotted.
  • Changed the data menu to be able to more easily find functions.
As always, if you have feedback I always appreciate getting it... all these ideas are based off suggestions that have come through from other teachers.
Access NZGrapher here: jake4maths.com/grapher and more details on NZGrapher can be found on MathsNZ and video tutorials on MathsNZ Students
