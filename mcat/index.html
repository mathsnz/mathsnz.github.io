Contacting NZQA and Others

If you would like a starting point to write a letter to NZQA, the ministry, the Minister for Education or the Shadow Minister for Education from Labour, Sandy Jones from Wellington Girls' College has written some excellent starting points. It is really important that you customise the letter before you send it though, as that way they cannot send a form response. At the moment NZQA and the Ministry believe this is an isolated incident that only affected a small number of schools.... we need to change this view.
